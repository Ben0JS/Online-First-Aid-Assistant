Why First Aid is Important

Respond quickly in emergencies
Reduce the severity of injuries
Save lives before professional help arrives
Stay calm and take control during a crisis

❤️ CPR Instructions

1. Check responsiveness.
2. Call 108 immediately.
3. Start chest compressions at 100–120 per minute.
4. Continue until help arrives.

💊 Medicine Kit Checklist

  • Bandages
  • Antiseptic
  • Cotton
  • Painkillers
  • Scissors

🧯 Fire Safety Tips

  • Stop, Drop, and Roll if on fire
  • Stay low to avoid smoke
  • Use a fire extinguisher if safe
  • Evacuate immediately and call 101
